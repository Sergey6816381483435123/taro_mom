Three-card Tarot spread FAQ

What is a three-card Tarot spread?

It is a compact reading that uses three cards to examine a situation from several sides and interprets both the individual cards and how they work together.

Does the AI interpret each card separately?

The bot considers each card, but it also connects their positions, orientations, combinations, and the context of your question into one coherent interpretation.

Can I continue after the three-card reading?

Yes. You can draw an additional card or ask a follow-up question, and the bot keeps the context of the original question and spread.

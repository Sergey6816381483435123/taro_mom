Questions and answers

AI Tarot bot FAQ

Clear answers about readings, interpretation, credits, and supported languages.

What is the Taroshenka Tarot bot?

Taroshenka is an AI-powered Tarot reading bot in Telegram. You choose a spread, ask a question, and receive card images with a connected interpretation in the chat.

How does the AI Tarot interpretation work?

The bot considers each card, its position, its upright or reversed orientation, and the context of your question. It then combines those elements into one readable interpretation.

Which Tarot readings are available?

Available formats include a Daily Card, Yes or No, a three-card spread, Dawn, a five-card relationship reading, and Premium readings such as the ten-card Celtic Cross and twelve-card Year Ahead.

Does the bot predict the future?

No. Tarot readings and AI interpretations are intended for entertainment and self-reflection. They offer a symbolic perspective rather than a guaranteed prediction.

How do credits and Premium work?

Credits are used for paid readings and additional actions. Packages of 5, 10, or 30 credits and a monthly Premium option are available; current prices and payment methods are shown in the bot before purchase.

Which languages does the Tarot bot support?

The bot supports English, Russian, German, Spanish, French, Hindi, Portuguese, and Turkish. You can change the saved language in your profile settings.

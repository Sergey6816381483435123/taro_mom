Yes or No Tarot FAQ

How does the Yes or No Tarot reading work?

You ask a clear question in the Telegram bot. One card is drawn, and the bot explains its symbolic direction and meaning in the context of that question.

Does Yes or No Tarot predict a certain outcome?

No. The reading offers a symbolic perspective for entertainment and self-reflection, not a reliable prediction or a substitute for professional advice.

Where do I receive the reading?

The complete flow takes place in the Taroshenka Telegram bot, including the card image and AI interpretation.

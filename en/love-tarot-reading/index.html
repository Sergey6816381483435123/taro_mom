What does the relationship reading explore?

Taroshenka's relationship format uses five cards to look at the emotional dynamics between people. It can help you reflect on feelings, expectations, obstacles, hidden influences, and possible direction without claiming to read another person's mind.

Love Tarot reading FAQ

How many cards are in the relationship Tarot reading?

The relationship reading uses five cards to explore emotional dynamics, expectations, obstacles, hidden influences, and possible direction.

Can Tarot reveal exactly what another person thinks?

No. The reading is a symbolic interpretation of relationship dynamics, not a reliable way to read another person's thoughts or guarantee an outcome.

How does AI interpret the relationship spread?

The bot considers each card, its position and orientation, the other cards, and the context of your question to create one connected interpretation.
